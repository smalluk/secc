Sponsorship

We currently have a Club sponsor, Emsleys Estate Agents who have kindly sponsored us for over ten years now. This income allows us to keep our grounds equipment running! We also have various match ball sponsors who donate a minimum of £20 to cover the cost of the balls we use in a match. If you would like to be a match ball sponsor please get in touch.
